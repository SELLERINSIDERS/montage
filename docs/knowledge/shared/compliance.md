## Allowed (structure/function claims)
- "Supports healthy sleep patterns*"
- "Promotes a calm, relaxed state*"
- "Helps maintain healthy stress response*"
- Always add asterisk (*) + FDA disclaimer

## Banned
- **Disease claims**: "cures insomnia", "treats anxiety", "prevents depression"
- **Drug claims**: "clinically proven to treat/cure/prevent"
- **Absolute claims**: "guaranteed", "100%", "always works"
- **Superiority claims**: "the best", "only one", "stood out", "checked every box"
- **Personal Attributes trap**: YOU + NEGATIVE STATE = instant Meta rejection
  - Bad: "Are you stressed all the time?"
  - Good: "Millions are rediscovering what calm actually feels like"

## Advertorial-Specific
- Never mention product name, pricing, offers, or specific mg doses in ads
- No melatonin attacks (melatonin is product #3 in our comparison — grogginess as criterion only)
- Curiosity hooks only — never reveal the winner
